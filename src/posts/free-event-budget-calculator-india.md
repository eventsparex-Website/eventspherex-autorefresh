## What Does the Budget Calculator Do?

Our calculator gives you an **instant, detailed cost breakdown** for any type of event in India. Just fill in four simple details:

-   **Event Type** — Exhibition Stall, Corporate Event, Product Launch, Conference, Wedding, Brand Activation, or Concert
-   **Number of Guests** — From intimate 50-person meetings to 50,000-capacity events
-   **City** — Mumbai, Delhi NCR, Bangalore, Chennai, Hyderabad, Kolkata, Pune, Ahmedabad, or any other city
-   **Duration** — 1 day to 1 week

Then pick your **budget level** — Basic (Economy), Standard (Mid-Range), Premium (High-End), or Luxury (Top-Tier) — and hit **Calculate**.

## Built for India's Event Industry

This isn't a generic calculator pulled from a Western template. Every rate in our tool is based on **real Indian market data**:

-   **City-wise pricing** — Mumbai and Delhi cost 30-40% more than Kolkata or Ahmedabad. Our calculator adjusts automatically.
-   **Event-specific labels** — An exhibition shows "Stall Space Rental" and "Stall Design & Fabrication" instead of generic labels. A wedding shows "Banquet Hall" and "Decoration & Flowers."
-   **Multi-day discounts** — Venue, AV, and logistics costs scale realistically for multi-day events (not just a straight multiply).
-   **All amounts in ₹** — formatted in the Indian numbering system (lakhs and crores).
