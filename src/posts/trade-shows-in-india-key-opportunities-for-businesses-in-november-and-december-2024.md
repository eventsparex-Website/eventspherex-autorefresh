## 1\. India International Trade Fair (IITF) 

![](https://indiatradefair.com/iitf/images/ITPO%20logo%20full%20branding.svg)

**Dates:** November 14–27, 2024 

**Location:** Pragati Maidan, New Delhi 

### **Overview:** 

The **India International Trade Fair (IITF)** is one of the largest and most well-known trade events in India, attracting participants from various sectors, including textiles, consumer goods, electronics, healthcare, and more. This event serves as a platform for businesses to connect with a diverse audience, including international visitors, buyers, and investors.

### Opportunities for Businesses:

-   **B2B & B2C Engagement:** With a blend of business professionals and general consumers attending, exhibitors can leverage this event to target both B2B and B2C markets.
-   **International Exposure:** Businesses can connect with global delegates from over 30 countries, offering opportunities for collaborations and exports.
-   **Government & Corporate Contracts:** Exhibitors can showcase products and solutions directly to government and corporate buyers looking for innovative solutions.

## 2\. India Corr Expo

![](https://www.indiacorrexpo.com/content/dam/sitebuilder/rxin/india-corr/ICE.png/_jcr_content/renditions/original.image_file.270.141.file/786551535/ICE.jpg)

**Dates:** November 22–24, 2024

**Location:** Bombay Exhibition Centre, Mumbai 

### **Overview:** 

India Corr Expo is the country’s largest event for the corrugated packaging industry. This event showcases the latest innovations in packaging, including corrugated boxes, machinery, and other packaging solutions, offering a valuable platform for businesses in the manufacturing, logistics, and retail industries. 

### Opportunities for Businesses:

-   **Technology Showcase:** Exhibitors can demonstrate new packaging technologies, machinery, and sustainability initiatives.
-   **Industry Networking:** Businesses can network with industry experts, suppliers, and potential buyers looking for innovative packaging solutions.
-   **Sustainability Focus:** With growing demand for eco-friendly packaging, the event provides businesses with an opportunity to promote sustainable packaging solutions.

## 3\. ACETECH

![](https://acetechexpo.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/03/Acetech-logo.svg)

**Dates:** November 16–19, 2024

**Location:** Pragati Maidan, New Delhi

### **Overview:** 

ACETECH is India’s leading trade fair for architecture, construction, engineering, and building materials. This annual event is a hub for businesses in the construction and infrastructure sectors to showcase innovative products and solutions to top architects, engineers, real estate developers, and contractors.

### Opportunities for Businesses:

-   **Market Access:** Exhibitors gain exposure to a wide range of industry professionals, helping them enter new markets or strengthen existing relationships.
-   **Product Demos:** Businesses can demonstrate their latest innovations in building materials, smart technologies, and eco-friendly construction solutions.
