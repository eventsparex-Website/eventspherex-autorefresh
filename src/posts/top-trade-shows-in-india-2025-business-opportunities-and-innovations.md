As 2025 kicks off, businesses across industries are gearing up for India's most prominent trade shows and exhibitions. These events provide excellent opportunities for networking, showcasing innovations, and expanding business horizons. Here's a list of the top 10 trade shows in India, starting from January, to help you plan ahead and maximize your business's potential.

* * *

## 1\. PlastIndia 2025

-   **Industry:** Plastics & Polymers
-   **Overview:** PlastIndia is one of the largest exhibitions in the plastics industry. The event is known for displaying the latest innovations in polymer processing, packaging, and materials science. It brings together global exhibitors and Indian manufacturers, making it a crucial event for businesses in the plastics supply chain.
-   **Why Attend?** Businesses can explore cutting-edge machinery, sustainable plastics, and global business opportunities, making it an unmissable event for stakeholders in the industry.

* * *

## 2\. India ITME 2025

-   **Industry:** Textile Machinery
-   **Overview:** India International Textile Machinery Exhibition (India ITME) is a key global event for textile machinery. It showcases innovations in spinning, weaving, knitting, dyeing, and finishing machinery, driving advancements in the textile industry.
-   **Why Attend?** With over 1,800 exhibitors, this is a significant opportunity for businesses to discover the latest in textile technology and connect with suppliers worldwide.

* * *

## 3\. ELECRAMA 2025

-   **Industry:** Electrical & Electronics
-   **Overview:** ELECRAMA is a premier event for electrical and electronics industry stakeholders. The exhibition covers various sectors including automation, renewable energy, and smart technologies.
-   **Why Attend?** The event attracts companies looking to explore the latest trends in energy, power generation, transmission, and distribution.

* * *

## 4\. India International Leather Fair (IILF)

-   **Industry:** Leather & Accessories
-   **Overview:** IILF is a leading leather and accessories event, featuring a wide range of products including leather goods, footwear, and fashion accessories.
-   **Why Attend?** It offers an excellent platform for B2B meetings, export opportunities, and exploring new markets in leather and footwear.

* * *

## 5\. ACETECH 2025

-   **Industry:** Architecture, Building & Construction
-   **Overview:** ACETECH is a leading trade show for architecture, construction, and design. It showcases the latest innovations in building materials, engineering, and interior design.
-   **Why Attend?** Architects, builders, and designers can explore groundbreaking construction technologies and network with industry leaders.

* * *

## 6\. Aero India 2025

-   **Industry:** Aerospace & Defence
-   **Overview:** Aero India is India's flagship aerospace and defense event, attracting global players in the defense industry. The exhibition showcases cutting-edge aerospace technology and provides opportunities for collaborations.
-   **Why Attend?** This is the perfect platform for defense and aerospace companies to explore partnerships and government contracts.

* * *

## 7\. India International Jewellery Show (IIJS) 2025

-   **Industry:** Gems & Jewellery
-   **Overview:** IIJS is one of the largest gems and jewellery exhibitions in Asia, drawing exhibitors and buyers from across the world.
-   **Why Attend?** The event is ideal for retailers, designers, and wholesalers looking to expand their business and discover new trends in gems and jewellery.

* * *

## 8\. DefExpo 2025

-   **Industry:** Defense & Security
-   **Overview:** DefExpo is India's biennial defense exhibition that showcases cutting-edge military equipment and defense technology. The event fosters collaborations between global defense manufacturers and the Indian armed forces.
-   **Why Attend?** DefExpo offers insights into defense innovation, military partnerships, and India's strategic defense objectives.

* * *

## 9\. India Wood 2025

-   **Industry:** Woodworking & Furniture
-   **Overview:** India Wood is Asia’s largest trade fair for the woodworking and furniture manufacturing industries. It showcases the latest machinery, raw materials, and innovative technologies.
-   **Why Attend?** Companies involved in furniture manufacturing, interior design, and woodworking machinery should not miss this opportunity to explore industry advancements.

* * *

## 10\. International Railway Equipment Exhibition (IREE) 2025

-   **Industry:** Railways & Transportation
-   **Overview:** IREE is Asia’s largest event for the railway industry, bringing together rail technology providers, manufacturers, and government bodies.
-   **Why Attend?** Businesses can explore technological advancements, network with key players in the railway sector, and discover investment opportunities in the expanding rail infrastructure.
