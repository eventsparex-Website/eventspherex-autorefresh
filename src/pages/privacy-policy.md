## Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** March 13, 2026

EventSphereX ("we", "us", or "our") operates the website [eventspherex.com](https://eventspherex.com). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website.

By using EventSphereX, you agree to the terms described in this policy.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Newsletter Signup:** Email address when you subscribe to our newsletter.
- **Contact Forms:** Name, email, phone number, and message content when you reach out to us.
- **Account Registration:** Name, email, and password if you create a Pro membership account.

### Information Collected Automatically
- **Usage Data:** Pages visited, time spent, referral source, and general browsing behavior.
- **Device Information:** Browser type, operating system, screen resolution, and device type.
- **IP Address:** Collected for analytics and security purposes.

## How We Use Your Information

- To deliver and improve our content, tools, and services
- To send newsletters and industry updates you have subscribed to
- To respond to your inquiries and support requests
- To analyze website traffic and user behavior for improvements
- To display relevant advertisements
- To prevent fraud and ensure website security

## Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

- Remember your preferences (such as dark mode settings)
- Analyze website traffic via third-party analytics services
- Serve and measure advertisements

You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect certain features of the website.

We use the following third-party services that may collect data:

These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:
