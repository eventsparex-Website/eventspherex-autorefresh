## Refund Policy

**Effective Date:** March 16, 2026

This Refund Policy applies to paid Pro membership subscriptions purchased on [eventspherex.com](https://eventspherex.com).

## 7-Day Refund Window

If you are not satisfied with your Pro membership, you may request a full refund within **7 days** of your purchase date. No questions asked.

After 7 days, subscription payments are non-refundable for the current billing period. You will continue to have access to Pro features until the end of your billing cycle.

## How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, contact us with the following details:

- Your registered email address
- Date of purchase
- Reason for the refund (optional, but helps us improve)

**Email:** [contact@eventspherex.com](mailto:contact@eventspherex.com)
**WhatsApp:** [+91 84849 81833](https://wa.me/918484981833)

## Processing Time

- Refund requests are reviewed within **2 business days**.
- Approved refunds are processed to the original payment method within **5-7 business days**, depending on your bank or payment provider.

## Cancellation

You can cancel your Pro membership at any time from your account settings. Cancellation stops future billing but does not trigger an automatic refund. If you are within the 7-day window, please contact us separately to request a refund.

## Exceptions

Refunds are not available for:

- Requests made after the 7-day refund window
- Accounts that have violated our [Terms of Service](/terms/)
- Promotional or discounted subscriptions, unless otherwise stated at the time of purchase
