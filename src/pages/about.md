## About EventSphereX

EventSphereX is India's leading event industry media portal, dedicated to empowering event professionals with the news, insights, and tools they need to succeed.

Founded by industry professionals who understand the challenges and opportunities in India's rapidly growing event sector, EventSphereX has become the go-to resource for thousands of professionals across the country.

## Our Mission

To inform, connect, and empower India's event industry by delivering timely news, expert insights, practical tools, and a thriving professional community.

## What We Cover

- **Exhibitions & Trade Shows** -- India's booming exhibition landscape, from mega trade fairs to niche industry expos
- **Corporate Events & Conferences** -- Summits, product launches, town halls, and corporate gatherings
- **MICE** -- Meetings, Incentives, Conferences, and Exhibitions driving business tourism
- **Weddings & Social Events** -- Trends, innovations, and business insights from India's wedding industry
- **Brand Activations & Experiential Marketing** -- Campaigns, pop-ups, and immersive brand experiences
- **Event Technology** -- The latest platforms, tools, and innovations shaping the industry

## By the Numbers

- **10,000+** monthly readers
- **200+** articles and insights published
- **50+** cities covered across India
- **5+** free tools for event professionals

## Who We Serve

Event planners, venue managers, exhibitors, caterers, decorators, AV professionals, brand managers, and everyone who makes events happen in India.

## Behind EventSphereX

EventSphereX is a product of **Overwrite Design Agency**, founded by **Manoj Sharma**. With deep roots in India's event ecosystem, our editorial team brings firsthand industry experience to every story we publish.

We believe that access to quality information should not be a barrier to professional growth. That is why our content, tools, and resources are freely available to the community.
